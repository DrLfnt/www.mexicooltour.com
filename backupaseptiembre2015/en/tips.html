Historic Center

Mexico city better known as the city of palaces and ancient capital of the Aztec empire. It is a vibrant megalopolis, home of more than 22 millions inhabitants, it was founded by the Aztecs in 1325 and considered the oldest capital city of the continent.

Memories of Mexico

Rotunda of the illustrious people is without a doubt the most important cementery of Mexico. Located in Chapultepec park, is an oasis of peace in the midle of the City, this contains tombs of personalities, such as:
Diego Rivera, David Alfaro Siqueiros, José Clemente Orozco, Jaime Nuno, etc.
