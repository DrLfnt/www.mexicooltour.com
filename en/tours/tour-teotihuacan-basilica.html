Teotihuacan

Mystery at Prodigy.

Embark on an adventure into the mythical city of the gods, considered the largest and most powerful of Mesoamerica: Teotihuacán; named for the Aztecs, where according to their myths and religion gods were created. Understand their worldview represented in his monumental constructions such as:

  • The carriageway of the Dead
  • Pyramid of the Moon
  • Quetzalpapalotl's Palace
  • Pyramid of the Sun

Basilica

Power faith and devotion.

Then let us be guided towards the most important shrine in Mexico: "La Villa de Guadalupe" Where occurred the apparitions of the Virgin Mary of Guadalupe to Juan Diego on the hill of Tepeyac in 1531.

In this important congress visit the old minor basilica, the temple of the hole dug and modern Basilica; place that protects the image of the brunette virgin which is the object of worship and veneration.

Undoubtedly an unforgettable experience.

  • Bilingual guide - certified by the Ministry of Tourism of Mexico (SECTUR)
  • Free maps
  • All admissions to archaelogical sites are included
