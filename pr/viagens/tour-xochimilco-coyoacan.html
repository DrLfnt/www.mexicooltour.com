Xochimilco Canals & Coyoacan

Charming Places: Rustling, Color & Love.

Coyoacan, Without a doubt is one of the most traditional and romantic neighbourhood of Mexico city. Let you captivate by its ancient constructions as Blue House (Frida Kahlo Museum), house of Hernan Cortes and Saint John the Baptist Church.

Be witness of historical events. Personalities as Octavio Paz (Nobel prize of literature), Dolores del Rio, Emilio "el indio Fernadez", Leon Trostky and the famous painter Frida Kahlo, left footprints and memories in the mexican history.

Ride on a traditional trajinera boat along the Xochimilco canals and discover how was the Aztec city that astonished the spaniards, built over the lake Texcoco and see how they inhabited in man made islands. Xochimilco was declared by the UNESCO as World Heritage Site of Mankind.

  • Comfortable transportation with a/C in vans or bus depending of the number of passengers
  • Bilingual guide - certified by the Ministry of Tourism of Mexico (SECTUR)
  • Free maps
  • All admissions to archaelogical sites are included

  • Services not mentioned above
  • Food and drinks
